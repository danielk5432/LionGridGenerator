🦁 Lions & Contamination

🦁 Lion 🦠 Contamination

Instructions

  1. Click Nodes to place 🦁 (can place multiple lions in one node)
  2. After clicking Start, click and drag from a 🦁 node to draw a directional arrow.
  3. Click [Move Lions] button to move all 🦁 simultaneously.
  4. Then, 🦠 will spread to adjacent nodes by 1 step. It does not spread to nodes with 🦁.
